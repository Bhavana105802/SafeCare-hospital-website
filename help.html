Frequently Asked Questions

What are the visiting hours at SafeCare Hospital?

Our general visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily. However, visiting hours may vary for different departments such as ICU, NICU, and post-operative care units. Please check with the specific department or call our helpline for detailed information.

How do I find a doctor at SafeCare Hospital?

You can find a doctor at SafeCare Hospital in several ways:

  • Use the "Find a Doctor" feature on our website to search by specialty, name, or location
  • Call our appointment helpline at +91 1234 567 890
  • Visit any of our branches and inquire at the information desk
  • Ask your primary care physician for a referral to a specialist at SafeCare Hospital
Is parking available at SafeCare Hospital?

Yes, all our branches have dedicated parking facilities for patients and visitors. Most branches offer free parking for the first 2 hours, with nominal charges thereafter. Valet parking services are also available at select locations for your convenience.

How can I provide feedback about my experience?

We value your feedback and continuously strive to improve our services. You can provide feedback in the following ways:

  • Fill out the feedback form available at all our branches
  • Use the contact form on our website
  • Email us at feedback@safecarehospital.com
  • Call our feedback hotline at +91 1234 567 892
How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment in several ways:

  • Online: Use our appointment booking system on our website
  • Phone: Call our appointment desk at +91 1234 567 890
  • In-person: Visit any of our branches and schedule at the appointment desk
  • Mobile App: Download our mobile app and book appointments on the go
How do I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

To reschedule or cancel an appointment, please use one of the following methods:

  • Log in to your account on our website and manage your appointments
  • Call our appointment desk at +91 1234 567 890
  • Use our mobile app to modify your appointment

We request that you notify us at least 24 hours in advance if you need to reschedule or cancel your appointment.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring the following items to your appointment:

  • Photo ID (Aadhar Card, PAN Card, Passport, etc.)
  • Insurance card (if applicable)
  • List of current medications
  • Previous medical records, test results, or imaging studies related to your condition
  • Referral letter from your doctor (if applicable)
  • Payment method for any co-pays or fees not covered by insurance
What insurance plans does SafeCare Hospital accept?

SafeCare Hospital accepts most major insurance plans, including:

  • Government health insurance schemes (CGHS, ECHS, etc.)
  • Private insurance providers (Star Health, HDFC ERGO, Bajaj Allianz, etc.)
  • Corporate health insurance plans
  • International insurance for overseas patients

Please contact our insurance desk at +91 1234 567 893 to verify if your specific insurance plan is accepted.

How does the billing process work?

Our billing process is designed to be transparent and hassle-free:

  • For outpatient services: Payment is collected at the time of service. We accept cash, credit/debit cards, and digital payment methods.
  • For inpatient services: An initial deposit is required at admission. Regular interim bills are generated, and the final bill is presented at discharge.
  • For insured patients: We offer direct billing to insurance companies for cashless treatment. You may need to pay for services not covered by your insurance.

Our billing department is available to answer any questions about your bill or insurance coverage.

What is the SafeCare Family Card and how does it work?

The SafeCare Family Card is our membership program that offers exclusive benefits:

  • Discounts on consultations, diagnostics, and procedures
  • Priority appointment booking
  • Free annual health check-ups for family members
  • Dedicated relationship manager
  • Special rates for preventive health packages

The card covers up to 4 family members and is valid for one year from the date of issue. You can apply for the Family Card online or at any of our branches.

How can I get an estimate of my medical costs?

We understand the importance of financial planning for medical care. You can get a cost estimate in several ways:

  • Contact our billing department at +91 1234 567 894 for a general estimate
  • For planned procedures, ask your doctor or their office staff for a detailed estimate
  • Use our online cost calculator for common procedures (available on our website)
  • For complex cases, our financial counselors can provide personalized estimates based on your treatment plan and insurance coverage

Please note that actual costs may vary based on your specific medical needs and complications that may arise during treatment.

How can I access my medical records?

You can access your medical records in the following ways:

  • Patient Portal: Register for our online patient portal to view your test results, visit summaries, and medication lists
  • In-Person Request: Visit the Medical Records Department at any of our branches with a valid ID proof
  • Written Request: Submit a signed authorization form by mail or email
  • Authorized Representative: A person authorized by you can request records on your behalf with proper documentation

Please note that processing medical record requests may take 3-5 working days. For urgent requests, please inform the Medical Records Department.

What should I do to prepare for surgery?

Preparation for surgery varies depending on the procedure, but general guidelines include:

  • Follow all pre-operative instructions provided by your surgeon
  • Complete all required pre-surgical tests and evaluations
  • Inform your doctor about all medications, supplements, and allergies
  • Stop eating and drinking as instructed before surgery (usually 8-12 hours prior)
  • Arrange for someone to drive you home after the procedure
  • Bring all necessary documents, including ID, insurance information, and advance directives
  • Remove jewelry, contact lenses, and dentures before surgery

Your surgical team will provide specific instructions tailored to your procedure and medical condition.

Our vaccination clinics are run by experienced healthcare professionals who can provide guidance on recommended vaccines based on your age, health status, and travel plans.

How can I manage my medications safely?

Safe medication management is crucial for effective treatment. Here are some guidelines:

  • Keep an updated list of all medications you take, including over-the-counter drugs and supplements
  • Take medications exactly as prescribed by your doctor
  • Use pill organizers if you take multiple medications
  • Set reminders for medication times
  • Store medications properly (away from heat, moisture, and direct sunlight)
  • Check expiration dates regularly and dispose of expired medications properly
  • Inform all your healthcare providers about all the medications you take to avoid drug interactions
  • Never share your prescription medications with others

Our pharmacy staff can provide personalized medication counseling to help you manage your medications effectively.
